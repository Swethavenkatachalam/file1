Spinach and Feta Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

1. Spread the spinach evenly on the tortilla wrap.

2. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the spinach.

3. Drizzle with olive oil.

4. Roll up the tortilla and cut it in half. Serve immediately.
