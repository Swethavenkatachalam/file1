Instant Ramen with Veggies

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack instant ramen
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (frozen or fresh)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Optional: boiled egg, sesame seeds

Instructions:

1. Cook ramen according to the package instructions, adding mixed vegetables during the last few minutes of cooking.

2. Stir in soy sauce to taste.

3. If desired, top with a boiled egg and sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
