Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of bread (toasted)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Optional: chili flakes, lemon juice

Instructions:

Mash the avocado in a bowl. Spread it on the toasted bread. Drizzle olive oil, sprinkle salt, pepper, and chili flakes. Optionally, add lemon juice for extra freshness.
