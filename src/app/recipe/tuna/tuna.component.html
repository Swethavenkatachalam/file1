Tuna Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 can tuna, drained
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, combine the drained tuna, mayonnaise, and chopped celery.

2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

3. Mix well and serve on its own or with crackers.
