Chinese Beef Stir Fry Recipe

Chinese-style Beef Stir Fry is a quick and flavorful dish, featuring tender beef slices stir-fried with vegetables in a savory sauce. The key to this dish is marinating the beef for tenderness and stir-frying at high heat to retain flavor. Hereâ€™s a recipe to create an authentic Chinese Beef Stir Fry.

Ingredients:

For the Beef:

  • 500g (1 lb) beef sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine (Chinese cooking wine)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

For the Stir Fry:

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil (or peanut oil)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienned (optional)
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets (optional)
  • 2 green onions, chopped for garnish

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (slurry for thickening)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp black or white pepper
  • 1/4 cup beef or chicken broth (or water)

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Marinate the Beef

Prepare the beef: Thinly slice the beef against the grain for tenderness.

Marinate the beef: In a bowl, mix the beef with soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, sesame oil, cornstarch, black pepper, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 15-20 minutes to enhance the flavors and tenderize the meat.

Step 2: Prepare the Sauce

Mix the sauce: In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce (optional), sugar, black or white pepper, sesame oil, and the cornstarch slurry. Stir well and set aside.

Step 3: Stir-Fry the Beef

Heat the oil: In a wok or large pan, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over high heat.

Cook the beef: Add the marinated beef to the hot wok in a single layer. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the beef is browned and just cooked through. Remove from the wok and set aside.

Step 4: Stir-Fry the Vegetables

Add aromatics: In the same wok, add a bit more oil if needed. Stir-fry the minced garlic and chopped ginger for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

Cook the vegetables: Add the sliced onions, bell peppers, and any additional vegetables (like carrots or broccoli). Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are crisp-tender but still vibrant.

Step 5: Combine Beef and Sauce

Return the beef: Add the cooked beef back into the wok with the vegetables.

Add the sauce: Pour the prepared sauce over the beef and vegetables, stirring to coat everything evenly. Let it simmer for 1-2 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken and the flavors to meld together.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

Garnish: Sprinkle chopped green onions on top before serving.

Serve: This beef stir fry pairs perfectly with steamed white rice, fried rice, or noodles.

Tips for Success:

  • Beef slicing: For easier slicing, freeze the beef for about 15 minutes before cutting. This helps you achieve thin slices that cook quickly and remain tender.
  • High heat: Stir-frying is best done over high heat to sear the meat and vegetables quickly, preserving their texture and flavor.
  • Customize vegetables: Feel free to use other vegetables like snap peas, mushrooms, or zucchini to personalize your stir fry.

This Chinese Beef Stir Fry is a delicious and balanced dish with tender beef, crisp vegetables, and a savory sauce, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal!
