Sesame Shrimp Stir-Fry Recipe

Sesame Shrimp Stir-Fry is a delicious Chinese-style dish featuring tender shrimp tossed in a flavorful sauce with a hint of sesame. It’s quick to prepare, with a light, savory, and slightly sweet taste. Here’s a recipe to create an authentic Sesame Shrimp Stir-Fry in Chinese style.

Ingredients:

For the Shrimp:

  • 500g (1 lb) shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil (or peanut oil)
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup snap peas (optional)
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets (optional)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (toasted)
  • 2 green onions, chopped for garnish

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (slurry for thickening)
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth (or water)

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Marinate the Shrimp

Prepare the shrimp: Peel and devein the shrimp, leaving the tails on if desired.

Marinate the shrimp: In a bowl, mix shrimp with soy sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, black pepper, and salt. Let it marinate for 10-15 minutes.

Step 2: Prepare the Sauce

Mix the sauce: In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce (optional), honey or sugar, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and the cornstarch slurry. Stir well and set aside.

Step 3: Stir-Fry the Shrimp

Heat the oil: In a wok or large pan, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.

Cook the shrimp: Add the marinated shrimp to the hot wok in a single layer. Stir-fry for about 2 minutes on each side, until the shrimp turn pink and are just cooked through. Remove the shrimp from the wok and set aside.

Step 4: Stir-Fry the Vegetables

Sauté aromatics: In the same wok, add a bit more oil if needed. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

Cook the vegetables: Add the sliced onions, bell peppers, snap peas, and broccoli florets. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender but still vibrant.

Step 5: Add the Sauce and Shrimp

Return the shrimp: Add the cooked shrimp back to the wok with the vegetables.

Pour in the sauce: Stir in the prepared sauce and toss everything to coat well. Let it simmer for 1-2 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly and coat the shrimp and vegetables.

Step 6: Garnish and Serve

Add sesame seeds: Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds over the stir-fry.

Garnish: Top with chopped green onions before serving.

Serve: Sesame Shrimp Stir-Fry is best served hot with steamed rice or noodles.

Tips for Success:

  • Don’t overcook the shrimp: Shrimp cook very quickly. Remove them from the heat as soon as they turn pink to prevent them from becoming rubbery.
  • High heat: Stir-frying over high heat ensures the vegetables stay crisp and vibrant, while the shrimp cook evenly.
  • Toasted sesame seeds: Toast the sesame seeds briefly in a dry pan to enhance their nutty flavor before adding them to the stir-fry.

This Sesame Shrimp Stir-Fry offers a wonderful balance of flavors, with the nuttiness of sesame, the freshness of vegetables, and the sweetness of shrimp, making it a perfect dish for a quick and healthy meal!
