Dan Dan Noodles Recipe

Dan Dan Noodles is a classic Sichuan street food dish featuring noodles in a flavorful and spicy sauce with minced pork, sesame paste, chili oil, and Sichuan peppercorns. Hereâ€™s an authentic Dan Dan Noodles recipe in a traditional Chinese style:

Ingredients:

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp sesame paste (or tahini)
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Chinese black vinegar (or rice vinegar)
  • 2 tbsp chili oil with sediment (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Sichuan peppercorns, ground
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-2 tsp doubanjiang (Sichuan broad bean chili paste) (optional for extra heat)
  • 1/4 cup chicken or vegetable broth (adjust for desired consistency)

For the Noodles:

  • 300g (10 oz) fresh or dried Chinese wheat noodles (or other thin noodles)

For the Topping:

  • 200g (7 oz) ground pork (or chicken)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine (or dry sherry)
  • 1 tbsp doubanjiang (Sichuan broad bean chili paste)
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns, ground
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped
  • Fresh cilantro or scallions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Crushed peanuts (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Sauce

In a small bowl, mix together the sesame paste, peanut butter (if using), soy sauce, black vinegar, chili oil, sesame oil, sugar, minced garlic, and ground Sichuan peppercorns. Add the chicken or vegetable broth and stir until smooth and well combined. Set the sauce aside.

Step 2: Cook the Pork Topping

Heat the vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the ground pork and cook until it starts to brown, breaking it up into small pieces. Add the soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, doubanjiang, and ground Sichuan peppercorns. Stir and cook until the pork is cooked through and well-seasoned, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 3: Cook the Noodles

Bring a large pot of water to a boil and cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Fresh noodles will take just a couple of minutes, while dried noodles may take 4-5 minutes. Drain the noodles and set aside.

Step 4: Assemble the Dan Dan Noodles

Divide the prepared sauce between serving bowls. Add the cooked noodles on top of the sauce. Top the noodles with the cooked pork mixture.

Step 5: Garnish and Serve

Garnish the noodles with chopped green onions, fresh cilantro, and, if desired, crushed peanuts. Toss everything together to coat the noodles in the sauce and enjoy!

Tips for Success:

  • Adjust spice level: Increase or decrease the chili oil and doubanjiang according to your heat tolerance.
  • Balance flavors: Taste the sauce and adjust the salt, sugar, and vinegar to balance the salty, sweet, sour, and spicy elements.
  • Broth consistency: You can adjust the amount of broth for a thinner or thicker sauce, depending on your preference.

This Dan Dan Noodles recipe brings the bold flavors of Sichuan cuisine to your table with its signature spicy, numbing, and savory taste! Enjoy!
