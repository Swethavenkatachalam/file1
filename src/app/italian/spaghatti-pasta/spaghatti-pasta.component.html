Vermicelli alla Mediterranea

This Italian-style vermicelli pasta dish is prepared simply with fresh ingredients, highlighting the lightness and texture of the vermicelli noodles. It balances the mild flavors of garlic, olive oil, tomatoes, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 400g (14 oz) vermicelli pasta
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 250g (9 oz) cherry tomatoes, halved (or peeled and chopped Roma tomatoes)
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
  • Grated Parmesan or Pecorino Romano cheese (optional)
  • Lemon zest (optional, for freshness)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Step 1: Cook the Vermicelli
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    2. Add the vermicelli and cook according to the package instructions until al dente (usually 5-7 minutes).
    3. Reserve about 1/2 cup of the pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta.
  2. Step 2: Prepare the Tomato and Garlic Sauce
    1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    2. Add the sliced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant and golden, but be careful not to burn it.
    3. Add the halved cherry tomatoes (or chopped Roma tomatoes) to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes until they soften and release their juices.
    4. Season with a pinch of salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Let the sauce simmer for 5-6 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Combine Pasta and Sauce
    1. Add the cooked vermicelli directly to the skillet with the tomato and garlic sauce.
    2. Toss the pasta in the sauce, ensuring it's well coated. If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta water to thin it out.
  4. Step 4: Season and Garnish
    1. Stir in the chopped basil and parsley for fresh, aromatic flavor.
    2. Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.
    3. Optionally, sprinkle grated Parmesan or Pecorino Romano cheese and add lemon zest for brightness.
  5. Step 5: Serve
    1. Transfer the vermicelli to plates or a large serving dish.
    2. Drizzle a little more extra virgin olive oil over the top for added richness, and serve immediately.

Tips for Success

  • Use fresh ingredients: The simplicity of this dish relies on the freshness of the tomatoes, garlic, and herbs.
  • Cook pasta al dente: Vermicelli is a thin pasta, so keep an eye on it to avoid overcooking.
  • Add proteins: You can add shrimp, chicken, or anchovies for a heartier dish.
