Fettuccine with Prawns (Fettuccine ai Gamberi)

This dish combines the rich flavors of prawns with a creamy, garlicky sauce over perfectly cooked fettuccine.

Ingredients

  • 400g (14 oz) fettuccine
  • 400g (14 oz) large prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine (optional)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional for a creamy version)
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Step 1: Cook the Fettuccine
    1. Boil water: In a large pot, bring water to a boil, and add a generous amount of salt.
    2. Cook the pasta: Add the fettuccine and cook according to the package instructions until al dente, usually about 8-10 minutes. Stir occasionally.
    3. Reserve pasta water: Reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta cooking water before draining.
    4. Drain the pasta: Drain the fettuccine and set aside.
  2. Step 2: Cook the Prawns
    1. Prepare the prawns: Season the peeled and deveined prawns with salt, pepper, and a pinch of lemon zest.
    2. Sauté the prawns: In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil over medium heat. Add the prawns and cook for 2-3 minutes per side until pink and fully cooked. Remove the prawns from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Step 3: Prepare the Sauce
    1. Sauté the garlic: In the same skillet, add the remaining olive oil and minced garlic. Cook on medium heat for about 1 minute until fragrant, being careful not to burn the garlic.
    2. Deglaze with white wine (optional): If using, pour in the white wine to deglaze the pan. Let it simmer for 1-2 minutes until reduced slightly.
    3. Add cream (optional): For a creamy version, stir in the heavy cream and simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
    4. Add lemon juice and zest: Stir in the lemon juice and a pinch of lemon zest for brightness.
  4. Step 4: Combine Pasta, Sauce, and Prawns
    1. Add the fettuccine: Toss the drained fettuccine into the skillet with the sauce. Add a little reserved pasta water to help bind the sauce to the pasta.
    2. Add the prawns: Return the cooked prawns to the skillet and toss to combine everything. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to heat through.
  5. Step 5: Season and Garnish
    1. Adjust seasoning: Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using).
    2. Add parsley: Stir in the chopped fresh parsley for a touch of color and freshness.
  6. Step 6: Serve
    1. Serve immediately: Transfer the fettuccine and prawns to individual plates or a large serving bowl.
    2. Garnish: Optionally, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and finish with an extra drizzle of olive oil or lemon zest before serving.

Tips for Success:

  • Don’t overcook the prawns: Prawns cook quickly, so avoid overcooking them to keep them tender.
  • Quality ingredients: Use fresh prawns and high-quality olive oil for the best flavor.
  • Creamy or not: You can skip the cream for a lighter, more traditional version or keep it for a richer, indulgent sauce.
