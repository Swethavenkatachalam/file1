Carnitas de Pollo

Carnitas de Pollo (Mexican-style chicken carnitas) is a flavorful and versatile dish made with tender, shredded chicken cooked in a rich blend of spices. Perfect for tacos, burritos, or enjoyed on its own with sides. Here’s how to make this dish Mexican-style.

Ingredients:

For the Chicken:

  • 500g (1 lb) chicken thighs (bone-in, skinless)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth (or water)
  • 1/4 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

For Serving:

  • Warm tortillas (flour or corn)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Diced onions
  • Sliced radishes
  • Lime wedges
  • Salsa or pico de gallo

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Season the chicken: Pat the chicken thighs dry with paper towels and season them with salt and pepper on both sides.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

  1. Sauté aromatics: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion is translucent, about 2-3 minutes.
  2. Brown the chicken: Add the chicken thighs to the skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side until browned.
  3. Add spices: Sprinkle the cumin, chili powder, paprika, dried oregano, smoked paprika (if using), and cayenne pepper (if using) over the chicken. Stir to coat the chicken evenly with the spices.
  4. Add liquids: Pour in the chicken broth and fresh orange juice. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

Step 3: Shred the Chicken

  1. Shred the chicken: Remove the chicken thighs from the skillet and place them on a cutting board. Use two forks to shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces. Return the shredded chicken to the skillet.

Step 4: Reduce the Sauce

  1. Simmer to thicken: Increase the heat to medium-high and cook the chicken uncovered for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the liquid reduces and thickens slightly. Stir occasionally to ensure the chicken doesn’t stick to the pan.

Step 5: Finish the Carnitas

  1. Add lime juice: Stir in the lime juice for a fresh, tangy flavor. Adjust seasoning with additional salt and pepper if needed.

Step 6: Serve

  1. Prepare the tortillas: Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  2. Assemble: Serve the shredded chicken carnitas in warm tortillas, topped with fresh cilantro, diced onions, sliced radishes, and a squeeze of lime juice. Add salsa or pico de gallo for extra flavor.

Tips for Success:

  • Chicken thighs: Using bone-in, skinless chicken thighs will give the carnitas a richer flavor and more moisture. You can use boneless thighs for convenience if preferred.
  • Crispy texture: For a crispy texture, you can spread the shredded chicken in a single layer on a baking sheet and broil it for a few minutes until the edges are crispy.
  • Flavor variations: Feel free to experiment with additional spices like ground coriander or a splash of beer for a different flavor profile.

Carnitas de Pollo is a flavorful and versatile dish that brings a taste of Mexican cuisine to your table, perfect for tacos, burritos, or simply enjoyed with your favorite sides. Enjoy!
