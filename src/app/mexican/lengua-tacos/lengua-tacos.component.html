Lengua Tacos (Tacos de Lengua)

Lengua Tacos (Tacos de Lengua) are a beloved Mexican dish made from beef tongue that’s slow-cooked until tender, then shredded and served in warm tortillas. The result is a rich, flavorful, and tender filling perfect for tacos. Here’s a traditional recipe for making Lengua Tacos in Mexican style.

Ingredients:

For the Lengua:

  • 1 beef tongue (about 1.5-2 lbs)
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Water (enough to cover the tongue)

For the Tacos:

  • Warm tortillas (corn or flour)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges
  • Salsa or pico de gallo
  • Radishes, sliced (optional)
  • Avocado slices (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Lengua

  1. Clean the tongue: Rinse the beef tongue under cold water. Place it in a large pot.
  2. Add aromatics: Add the halved onion, crushed garlic, bay leaves, ground cumin, dried oregano, paprika, salt, and black pepper to the pot.
  3. Cook the tongue: Cover the tongue with water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 2-3 hours, or until the tongue is very tender and can be easily shredded.

Step 2: Peel and Shred the Tongue

  1. Cool and peel: Once the tongue is tender, remove it from the pot and let it cool slightly. Peel off the outer layer of skin (it should come off easily). Discard the skin and any excess fat.
  2. Shred the meat: Shred the meat using two forks or by hand. Return the shredded meat to the pot with some of the cooking liquid to keep it moist. You can also add additional seasonings or a bit of the reserved cooking liquid if desired.

Step 3: Prepare the Toppings

  1. Chop onion and cilantro: Finely chop the onion and cilantro. Set aside for taco assembly.
  2. Prepare other toppings: Slice the radishes and avocado if using. Prepare lime wedges and salsa or pico de gallo.

Step 4: Assemble the Tacos

  1. Warm the tortillas: Heat the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until warm and pliable.
  2. Fill the tacos: Place a generous amount of the shredded lengua on each tortilla.
  3. Top and garnish: Top with chopped onion, fresh cilantro, and other desired toppings such as radishes, avocado slices, and salsa.

Step 5: Serve

  1. Serve hot: Serve the tacos with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the top. Enjoy them while they’re hot!

Tips for Success:

  • Tongue preparation: Ensure the tongue is cooked until it’s very tender. Under-cooked tongue can be tough and chewy.
  • Flavor adjustments: Feel free to adjust the seasoning to your taste. You can add additional spices or herbs if desired.
  • Moisture: Adding some of the cooking liquid to the shredded meat helps keep it moist and flavorful.

Lengua Tacos offer a unique and delicious taste of Mexican cuisine. The tender, flavorful meat combined with fresh toppings makes for an unforgettable taco experience. Enjoy!
